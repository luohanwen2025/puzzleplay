About Juice Merge

Juice Merge is a light, satisfying merge puzzle in the spirit of the classic watermelon game. You drop one fruit at a time into a tall play field. When two identical fruits touch, they squash together and pop into the next bigger fruit in the chain. Keep merging your way up the ladder - cherry, strawberry, grape, all the way to the big one - and rack up points as you go.

It's the kind of game that is easy to start and hard to put down. There is no clock, so you can take your time planning each drop, but the field fills up faster than you expect, and a single careless placement can start a chain you did not intend. Quick sessions, a satisfying pop on every merge, and that "just one more try" pull make it a perfect pick-up-and-play puzzle.

How to play

Goal. Merge matching fruits to earn points and climb the fruit chain as high as you can. The game ends if the stack crosses the top line, so the real goal is keeping the field under control for as long as possible.

Dropping fruit. Move the fruit at the top left and right, then drop it. Whatever you drop lands on top of the existing stack and settles with simple physics.

Merging. When two of the same fruit touch, they combine into the next fruit up the ladder. Later merges are worth far more points, so building toward the bigger fruits is where the real score lives.

Tips & strategy

Build in columns

Stack the same fruit in a vertical line so each new drop triggers a cascade of merges. Cascades are the fastest way to clear space and score.

Keep the center low

The middle is where the game is lost. Keep the center stack short and use the edges as a buffer so a single bad drop can't end your run.

Plan for the next fruit

Always glance at the upcoming piece before you drop. Placing for what is coming, not just what you hold, turns good scores into great ones.

Frequently asked questions

Is Juice Merge really free? +

Yes. You can play the full game in your browser at no cost. No account, no payment, no download.

Does it work on my phone? +

Yes. The game adapts to touch and mouse, so it runs on iOS and Android phones and tablets, as well as laptops and desktops.

Do I need to install anything? +

No. It's an HTML5 game that loads directly in your browser. Just press play and it starts.

How do I score higher? +

Set up cascades by stacking identical fruit in a column, and aim for the bigger fruits late in the chain, which are worth far more points. See the tips above for the details.
